NeuroMap analyzes brain MRI scans with a convolutional neural network trained to distinguish glioma, meningioma, and pituitary tumors from healthy scans. Every prediction is paired with a Grad-CAM heatmap, highlighting exactly which region of the scan drove the model's decision.
